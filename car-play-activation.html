Apple CarPlay Activation (Lifetime)

NBTEvo System Apple CarPlay Activation. This is a Lifetime Activation of Apple CarPlay for BMW NBTEvo Systems.

Lifetime Activation of Apple CarPlay in BMWs equipped with NBTEvo iDrive systems.

With Apple CarPlay you are able to use your iPhone Apps on your iDrive screen, such as Google Maps, Waze, Spotify, Apple Music and much more. You are also able to use Siri while driving, for a safer experience.

This is ideal for vehicles that already have Apple CarPlay preparation as an Extra, however it is still possible to be used on vehicles without Apple CarPlay preparation.
